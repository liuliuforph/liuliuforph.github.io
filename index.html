About me

I am a PhD student in the ECE department of The University of Texas at Austin. I am fortunately advised by Prof. Constantine Caramanis. My research interests are in statistical machine learning and large scale optimization.

Before moving to Austin, I received the BE degree from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2016.
